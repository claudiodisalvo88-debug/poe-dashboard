Research question:
What is the first admissible research-framing boundary for exploring future PoE formula candidates without defining formula candidates, formula syntax, weights, coefficients, scalar score, ranking, leaderboard, incentives, tokens, payout, economic allocation or implementation?

Context:

* M-001 Protocol Kernel is CLOSED / COMPLETED.
* M-002 is OPENED / RESEARCH-ONLY.
* RQ-023 established the formula candidate research opening boundary.
* PD-022 and PD-023 are validated constraints.
* Formula candidates are not yet defined.
* Final formula is not defined.
* Implementation remains blocked.

Purpose:
Define the boundary of what RQ-024 is allowed to investigate before any formula candidate can be proposed later.

Boundary definition:
RQ-024 may define only the research boundary that establishes which objects, evidence, separations, blocks and minimum conditions must exist before PoE may later consider a formula-candidate proposal.

Conditional rule:
RQ-024 is safe only if it remains:

* non-formular
* non-numeric
* non-comparative
* non-economic
* non-implementative

* stale evidence handling

* incomplete or non-attributable evidence handling

* cross-IE collapse blocking
