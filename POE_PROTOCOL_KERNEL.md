## Core idea

Proof of Energy is a protocol for recognizing verified energy contributions based on observed behavior, measured reduction, attribution, systemic value and reputation, without prematurely converting that reputation into score, ranking, token, payout or economic allocation.

## Validated kernel layers

1. Primitive inefficiencies
PoE V1 works on validated primitive inefficiency classes:
* IE-002 efficiency
* IE-003 balancing
* IE-005 flexibility

2. Contribution
Contribution is not any observed behavior.
Observed behavior becomes candidate contribution only when linked to verified reduction, attribution and systemic relevance.

3. Reduction
Reduction is measured and verified.
Reduction alone is not automatically value.

4. Value
Recognized value exists only after attribution and protocol safety checks.
Value is not reputation.

5. Reputation eligibility
Recognized value can become reputation-eligible only under validated gates.
Reputation eligibility is not final reputation.

6. Aggregated reputation evidence
Aggregated evidence is not score, ranking or incentive.

7. Reputation-ready evidence
Evidence can become reputation-ready only when bounded, non-stale, coherent and safe.

8. PoE Reputation object
PoE Reputation object is non-economic, multidimensional and non-ordering.
It is not formula, score, ranking, incentive, token or payout.

9. Formula-ready reputation structure
A reputation object can become formula-ready only as a bounded, source-referenced, non-stale, non-conflicting, anti-manipulation-safe, non-economic and non-ordering structure.

10. Admissible formula constraints
Any future formula research must preserve IE separation, boundary integrity, time-window integrity, evidence lineage, stale/conflict/capped/conditional handling and anti-manipulation safety.
It must not introduce formula, weights, scalar score, ranking, incentive, token, payout, economic allocation or implementation.

11. Formula candidate research opening boundary
PoE may open formula-candidate research only as a future research-only phase after repository-confirmed upstream validation and PM approval.
This boundary does not propose formula candidates.
